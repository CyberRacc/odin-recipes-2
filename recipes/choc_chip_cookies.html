Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 250g butter, softened
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 200g brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 5ml vanilla extract
  • 375g all-purpose flour
  • 5g baking soda
  • 5g salt
  • 300g chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the butter mixture and mix well.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until lightly golden brown.
  8. Cool on wire racks.
